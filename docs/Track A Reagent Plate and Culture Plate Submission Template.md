# Reagent Plate and Culture Plate Submission Template

Use this as a guide for submitting your reagent plate designs to Monomer Bio. At minimum we expect the following:

1. Ordered recipe for Compound Media (where multiple components are added together)  
2. Reagent Plate Layout  
3. Culture Stock Plate Layout  
   

## **Pre-Mix Recipes (for Monomer to prepare)**

**ADDITION ORDER MATTERS — Precipitation Prevention**

Add components in the numbered order shown below. Dissolve phosphate into bulk water/buffer **before** adding divalent cations (MgSO4, trace metals). MgSO4 and trace metals must always be added **last**.

### **A2 — LBv2-Rich (9.0 mL total, pipette in 8.5 mL)**

*No phosphate — no precipitation risk. Order is flexible.*

| Step | Stock Solution | Volume | Final Conc. |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| 1 | Sterile Water | 6.939 mL | — |
| 2 | NaCl (5 M) | 0.675 mL | 375 mM |
| 3 | Tryptone (100 mg/mL) | 0.900 mL | 10 mg/mL |
| 4 | Yeast Extract (100 mg/mL) | 0.450 mL | 5 mg/mL |
| 5 | KCl (2 M) | 18 µL | 4 mM |
| **6** | **MgSO4 (1 M)** | **18 µL** | **2 mM** |

### **A3 — Defined-Minimal (9.0 mL total, pipette in 8.5 mL)**

*Contains phosphate \+ MgSO4 — addition order is critical.*

| Step | Stock Solution | Volume | Final Conc. | Rationale |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| 1 | Sterile Water | 7.812 mL | — | Bulk solvent first |
| 2 | NaCl (5 M) | 0.495 mL | 275 mM | Inert salt |
| 3 | MOPS pH 7 (1 M) | 0.360 mL | 40 mM | Buffer — sets pH |
| 4 | K2HPO4 (1 M) | 36 µL | 4 mM | Phosphate into dilute solution |
| 5 | KH2PO4 (1 M) | 9 µL | 1 mM | Phosphate into dilute solution |
| 6 | (NH4)2SO4 (1 M) | 90 µL | 10 mM | Nitrogen — no precipitation risk |
| 7 | Glucose (100 mg/mL) | 0.180 mL | 2 mg/mL (0.2%) | Carbon source — inert |
| 8 | Trace metals (1000×) | 9 µL | 1× | Contains Fe³⁺, Mn²⁺ — add after phosphate diluted |
| **9** | **MgSO4 (1 M)** | **9 µL** | **1 mM** | **LAST — divalent cation after phosphate fully diluted** |
