Reagent Plate and Culture Plate Submission Template

Use this as a guide for submitting your reagent plate designs to Monomer Bio. At minimum we expect the following:

  • Ordered recipe for Compound Media (where multiple components are added together)
  • Reagent Plate Layout
  • Culture Stock Plate Layout
Note for Carter: Our design uses 3 pre-mixed base stocks (A1, A2, A3) that differ only in MOPS concentration — they are otherwise identical. Please let us know if preparing 3 composed media exceeds bandwidth and we can simplify.

Our base stocks are formulated at 1.38× working concentration. The robot adds 145 µL base + 25 µL NaCl stock + 10 µL glucose stock + 20 µL cells = 200 µL total per experiment well.

ADDITION ORDER MATTERS — Precipitation Prevention
